Our Story

Built on first-hand industry experience

Our business was founded on first-hand experience gained across the construction industry, working with clients, main contractors, and Tier 2 and Tier 3 supply chain partners. Throughout our careers, we repeatedly encountered the same challenge: project planning and controls were often overlooked, under-resourced, or introduced too late to provide real value.

We saw smaller and medium-sized contractors struggling to access specialist planning support, particularly for short-term or ad hoc scopes of work. In many cases, project programmes were developed by individuals without formal planning expertise, resulting in schedules that lacked contractual compliance, failed to reflect project realities, and were regularly rejected. This often led to avoidable delays, disputes, and increased commercial risk.

We also recognised that many projects were not set up for success from the outset. Without robust planning foundations, projects can quickly lose visibility of progress, encounter programme delays, and experience escalating costs. Effective planning should not simply be a reporting exercise—it should provide foresight, highlight risks and opportunities, and give project teams a clear visual roadmap for successful delivery.

Driven by these experiences, we established our consultancy with a clear purpose: to change the way project planning and controls support is delivered. We provide flexible, practical, and commercially focused planning expertise that helps organisations establish strong project foundations, meet contractual requirements, and make better-informed decisions throughout the project lifecycle.

Our goal is simple—to make professional project controls and planning support accessible to every project, regardless of size or complexity.

Our Vision

Project controls as the industry standard

A key part of our vision is supporting Tier 2 and Tier 3 contractors, as well as smaller organisations that may not have access to dedicated planning and project controls resources. Too often, projects with smaller scopes of work are delivered without the planning foundations, contractual compliance, and project controls needed to succeed. We believe every project, regardless of size, deserves the same level of professional planning support. By helping clients establish realistic programmes, robust reporting processes, and compliant project controls from the outset, we enable them to reduce delivery risk, avoid costly delays, and minimise exposure to contractual disputes, delay damages, and other commercial challenges.

We are committed to building strong partnerships with our clients, taking the time to understand their goals and making their objectives our own. By working collaboratively and proactively, we become an extension of the project team, providing the insight and support needed to navigate challenges and drive successful project delivery.

We thrive on complexity and embrace the challenges that every project presents. Our passion lies in finding practical solutions and delivering clarity where uncertainty exists. Above all, we believe that integrated Project Controls should not be considered an aspiration or a luxury—it should be the industry standard and a realistic expectation for every project, regardless of size or complexity.

Through expertise, innovation, and collaboration, we aim to make effective project planning and controls accessible, practical, and indispensable to successful project delivery.
